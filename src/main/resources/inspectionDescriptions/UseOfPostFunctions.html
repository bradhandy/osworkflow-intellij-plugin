Unhandled exceptions in post-functions could cause inconsistent workflow state. When processing a workflow, the order of execution is action>pre-functions, result>pre-functions, transition to next step, result>post-functions, action>post-functions. An unhandled exception in a post-functions will cause the workflow to transition to the next step and skip any remaining post-functions. This can cause inconsistent workflow state and leave possibly critical business logic unexecuted.
